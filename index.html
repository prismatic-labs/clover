Pressure, not prevalence

A 55% pressure index does not mean 55% of people are affected. It measures how much economic stress is bearing down on mental health systems and individuals in a region, relative to a pre-pandemic baseline. Higher pressure means more people at risk, longer wait times, and greater unmet need.

About Clover

Clover is an open data tool mapping how economic stressors flow through to regional mental health pressure. It draws on published meta-analyses, OECD economic data, and WHO social determinants frameworks to quantify the pathways from cost-of-living pressures to mental health outcomes.

Pressure indices are computed from FRED, OECD, World Bank, and BLS data, adjusted for each country's safety net strength and mental health infrastructure.
